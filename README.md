# Aria

A calm, offline-first music player for iPhone and Android.

Discover free tracks, save them to your library, and keep listening from the Lock Screen and Dynamic Island — even without a connection.

## What you can do

- **Discover** music and save tracks for offline listening  
- **Library** of saved songs, likes, and imports from your device  
- **Playlists** to shape what plays next  
- **Now Playing** with shuffle, repeat, and likes  
- **Lock Screen & Dynamic Island** controls on iOS  
- **Home screen widgets** for what’s playing  

## Built with

Flutter · Clean Architecture · BLoC  

Designed for a focused listening experience — not another crowded streaming feed.
